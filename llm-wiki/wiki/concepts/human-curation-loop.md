The **human curation loop** is the part of the LLM Wiki workflow owned by the user: choosing sources, asking questions, guiding emphasis, reviewing summaries, and deciding what matters.

## Role in the Source

The source is explicit that the user does not usually write the wiki by hand. Instead, the user curates source material and explores the domain while the LLM handles the repetitive maintenance work.

## Boundary

The human remains responsible for judgment. The LLM can maintain pages, but it should not replace decisions about which sources are worth ingesting or which interpretations are meaningful.
