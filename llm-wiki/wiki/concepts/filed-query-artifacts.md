**Filed query artifacts** are useful answers created during Q&A that become durable wiki pages.

## Role in the Source

The source argues that query answers can take forms such as comparisons, analyses, slide decks, charts, or canvases. When those answers are valuable, they should be filed back into the wiki instead of disappearing into chat history.

## Why It Matters

This turns exploration into a maintenance loop. Each good question can add structure to the wiki, strengthening [[persistent-compounding-artifact|Persistent Compounding Artifact]] and making future questions easier to answer.
