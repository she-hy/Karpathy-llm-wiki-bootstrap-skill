**LLM-owned wiki maintenance** is the claim that the agent should own the derived wiki layer: writing summaries, updating cross-references, filing pages, keeping indexes current, and checking for contradictions.

## Role in the Source

The source frames the LLM as the party that does the work humans usually abandon: bookkeeping across many files. It argues that the LLM can keep a wiki maintained because it can touch many pages in one pass and does not get tired of repetitive structure work.

## Relationship to Human Work

The human is still in charge of sourcing, exploration, and judgment. The LLM is the maintainer, not the final authority on what is important.
