A **persistent compounding artifact** is the source's phrase for what makes an LLM Wiki different from repeated retrieval: the wiki keeps getting richer as sources and questions accumulate.

## What Compounds

- Cross-references between pages
- Contradictions and revisions
- Source summaries
- Concept and entity pages
- Comparisons and synthesis
- Filed query artifacts

## Why It Matters

Because the wiki persists, later questions can start from maintained structure rather than reassembling context from raw documents each time.
