**Memex** is Vannevar Bush's 1945 vision of a personal, curated knowledge store with associative trails.

## Role in the Source

The source compares the LLM Wiki idea to Memex. The similarity is private, curated, connected knowledge. The difference is that the LLM can perform the maintenance work Bush's vision could not automate.
